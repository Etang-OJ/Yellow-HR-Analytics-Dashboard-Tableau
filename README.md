# Yellow-HR-Analytics-Dashboard

A Tableau Project on Workforce Insights, Salary Trends & Attrition Forecasting

## Project Overview

This project analyzes HR data from Yellow, a mid-sized organization, to uncover insights related to:

Workforce demographics

Salary distribution & income patterns

Department-level performance

Attrition trends & forecasting

Job role–specific analysis

The final output is an interactive Tableau dashboard that transforms raw HR data into clear visual insights for decision-making.

This project demonstrates skills in data preparation, chart development, business storytelling, and dashboard design.

## Objectives

The main goals of this project are to:

✔️ Identify Key HR KPIs

Including:

Attrition rate

Employee count

Total & average monthly income

Avg tenure

✔️ Analyze Income Trends

Monthly income over time

Salary variation by department

Salary distribution by age group

Job role income comparison

✔️ Forecast Workforce Trends

Predict future attrition patterns

Identify potential HR risks

✔️ Build a Professional Tableau Dashboard

Designed with a bold yellow + black theme featuring:

Scorecards

Donut charts

Line charts

Bar charts

Department summaries

Custom icons & layout

## Key Insights

Majority of employees fall within the 25–44 age range.

Males represent a larger portion of the workforce.

Attrition spikes in certain quarters, indicating seasonal turnover patterns.

Departments like Sales show higher attrition, while R&D shows higher tenure.

Managers and Research Directors earn significantly more than other roles.

Younger employees (<25) show lower income and higher turnover tendency.
